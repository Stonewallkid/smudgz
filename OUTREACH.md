### Full description
```
## What is Smudgz?

Smudgz transforms your browser into a collaborative canvas. Draw on any webpage with multiple brush styles - solid, spray, rainbow, or glow. But here's the twist: your drawings aren't just art. They become physical platforms.

A customizable stick figure character appears on the page. Use arrow keys to run and jump. The character bounces off website elements AND your drawings. Build bridges, create staircases, or just doodle while platforming across the internet.

## Multiplayer Magic

Everyone on the same URL sees each other in real-time. You can:
- Play TAG - one player becomes "it" and chases others
- Race on custom obstacle courses
- Collaborate on drawings
- React to each other's annotations

## The Story

I'm a 40-year-old dad who doesn't code for a living. Built this as a "what if" experiment - what if you could doodle on websites and then play on those doodles?

My high school daughter became my first tester. She showed her friends. They showed their friends. Before I knew it, kids at her school were drawing on homework and playing tag on Wikipedia.

No VC funding. No growth team. Just a fun idea that people seem to enjoy.

## Features

- Draw with solid, spray, dots, rainbow, or glow brushes
- Add text and emoji stickers
- Stick figure platformer with physics
- Real-time multiplayer (no invite codes needed - same URL = same room)
- Tag game with "NO TAG BACKS" cooldown
- Course builder with checkpoints, trampolines, and obstacles
- Character customization (colors, hats, accessories)
- Annotations with reactions and replies
- Works on (almost) any website

## Try it free

Smudgz is completely free. All features included. No ads.
```

## Tips for Each Platform

### Twitter/X DMs
- Keep it under 200 words
- Lead with what makes it interesting
- Don't ask for "coverage" - ask if they want to "try it"

### Instagram DMs
- Even shorter - they're reading on mobile
- Use the video demo link immediately
- Follow them first and engage with a post before DMing

### Email
- Subject line is everything
- First sentence should explain what it is
- Include the video demo early
- Make it easy to say yes

### Reddit
- Be genuine, not salesy
- Engage with every comment
- Don't post to multiple subs on the same day
- Read each sub's rules first

### Product Hunt
- Launch Tuesday-Thursday for best visibility
- Get 5-10 friends to upvote in first hour
- Respond to every comment quickly
- Maker badge = more trust
